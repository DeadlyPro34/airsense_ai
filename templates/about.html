About Us

Welcome to AirSense AI, your personal and intelligent air quality advisor.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help people make informed decisions about their daily activities by providing hyper-local, real-time air quality data coupled with AI-driven health advisories. We believe that everyone has a right to understand the air they breathe, especially sensitive groups like children, the elderly, and those with respiratory conditions.

How It Works

We fetch live air pollution data from OpenWeatherMap, converting complex pollutant metrics (like PM2.5, NO2, O3) into an easy-to-understand Air Quality Index (AQI).

Instead of just giving you a number, we use Groq's high-speed AI models (Llama 3) to generate actionable health advice. Crucially, all AI responses are strictly grounded in World Health Organization (WHO) guidelines to ensure safety and accuracy.

Privacy First

We believe in absolute privacy. We do not track your location, and we do not store your API keys on our servers. Everything is encrypted directly in your browser.
